# Soledgic Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 2024  
**Last Updated:** December 2024

---

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Osifo Holdings, L.L.C. ("Soledgic," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our accounting infrastructure service ("Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently. This policy applies to all users of Soledgic, including business customers and their end users (creators/contractors).

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Name and email address
- Company name and billing address
- Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
- Phone number (optional)

**Business Data:**
- Transaction records (amounts, dates, descriptions)
- Creator/contractor information (names, email addresses)
- Bank account identifiers (via Bank Feed tokens, not raw credentials)
- Payment processor data (Payment Processor webhook events)

**Tax Reporting Data:**
- Payment summaries by recipient ID (amounts only)
- No SSNs, EINs, TINs, names, or addresses stored
- You maintain recipient PII in your own systems

**Communications:**
- Support tickets and email correspondence
- Feedback and survey responses

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:**
- Features used and actions taken
- API calls and timestamps
- Error logs and performance data

**Device and Connection Data:**
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referring URLs

**Cookies and Similar Technologies:**
- Session cookies for authentication
- Preference cookies for settings
- Analytics cookies (optional, can be disabled)

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

**Payment Processor:**
- Webhook events (charges, refunds, payouts, disputes)
- Transaction metadata
- No payment card numbers (PCI data)

**Bank Feed:**
- Bank account balances and transactions
- Account and routing numbers (tokenized)
- Institution information

We do not receive or store your bank login credentials. Bank Feed handles authentication directly.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 To Provide the Service

- Process and record transactions
- Generate financial reports
- Reconcile bank and payment data
- Create tax documents (1099-K, 1099-NEC)
- Send transactional emails (receipts, alerts)

### 2.2 To Improve the Service

- Analyze usage patterns
- Identify and fix bugs
- Develop new features
- Optimize performance

### 2.3 To Communicate with You

- Respond to support requests
- Send service announcements
- Notify you of policy changes
- Marketing communications (with consent, opt-out available)

### 2.4 To Protect Our Service

- Detect and prevent fraud
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 With Your Consent

We share information when you explicitly authorize us to do so.

### 3.2 With Service Providers

We use trusted third parties to help operate our Service:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| Supabase | Database hosting | All Service data |
| Payment Processor | Payment processing | Billing information |
| Bank Feed | Bank connections | Bank account tokens |
| Resend | Email delivery | Email addresses |
| Vercel | Web hosting | Usage logs |

All providers are bound by data processing agreements.

### 3.3 For Legal Reasons

We may disclose information if required by law or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:
- Comply with legal process (subpoena, court order)
- Protect our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or security threats
- Protect the safety of any person

### 3.4 Business Transfers

If Soledgic is acquired, merged, or sells assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you of any such change.

### 3.5 Aggregated Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you (e.g., "X% of platforms use marketplace mode").

---

## 4. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Transaction records | 7 years after account closure |
| Tax payment summaries | 7 years after generation |
| Account information | Duration of account + 90 days |
| Usage logs | 12 months |
| Support tickets | 3 years |

After retention periods expire, data is permanently deleted or anonymized.

---

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Technical Measures

- **Encryption in transit:** TLS 1.3 for all connections
- **Encryption at rest:** AES-256 for stored data
- **Access controls:** Role-based permissions, API key authentication
- **Monitoring:** Real-time alerting for suspicious activity

### 5.2 Organizational Measures

- Limited employee access to production data
- Background checks for personnel with data access
- Regular security training
- Incident response procedures

### 5.3 No Sensitive Tax Information Stored

Soledgic does NOT store:
- Social Security Numbers (SSNs)
- Employer Identification Numbers (EINs)
- Names or addresses for tax purposes

We only store payment amounts by recipient ID. You maintain recipient PII in your own systems and merge with our payment data for 1099 filing.

### 5.4 Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:
- Notify you within 72 hours of discovery
- Describe the nature of the breach
- Explain steps we are taking
- Provide guidance on protecting yourself

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Portability

You can:
- Access your data through the dashboard
- Export all your data in CSV or JSON format
- Request a copy of personal data we hold about you

### 6.2 Correction

You can update your account information at any time through settings. For corrections to historical data, contact support.

### 6.3 Deletion

You can:
- Delete individual records through the Service
- Request account deletion (subject to retention requirements)
- Request deletion of specific personal data

Note: We may retain certain data as required by law (e.g., tax records for 7 years).

### 6.4 Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your data while we address a concern.

### 6.5 Object to Processing

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests. We will stop unless we have compelling grounds.

### 6.6 Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw at any time. This does not affect processing that occurred before withdrawal.

### 6.7 Marketing Opt-Out

You can opt out of marketing emails by:
- Clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email
- Adjusting preferences in account settings
- Contacting privacy@soledgic.com

### 6.8 Do Not Track

We do not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals.

## 8. Specific Provisions

### 8.1 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have additional rights:

**Right to Know:** You can request disclosure of:
- Categories of personal information collected
- Sources of collection
- Purpose of collection
- Categories of third parties we share with
- Specific pieces of personal information we hold

**Right to Delete:** You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to exceptions.

**Right to Opt-Out of Sale:** We do not sell personal information. If this changes, we will provide an opt-out mechanism.

**Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@soledgic.com or use our online form.

### 8.2 European Residents (GDPR)

Our legal bases for processing:
- **Contract:** To provide the Service you requested
- **Legitimate interests:** To improve our Service and prevent fraud
- **Legal obligation:** To comply with tax and regulatory requirements
- **Consent:** For optional features and marketing

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

### 8.3 Creator/Contractor Data

If you are a creator or contractor whose information was submitted by a Soledgic customer:
- The customer is the "data controller" for your data
- Soledgic is the "data processor" acting on their behalf
- Contact the customer first regarding your data rights
- You may also contact us at privacy@soledgic.com

## 9. Bank Feed Data Handling

### 9.1 What Bank Feed Accesses

When you connect a bank account via Bank Feed:
- Account balances
- Transaction history
- Account and routing numbers (tokenized)

### 9.2 What We Store

- Bank Feed access tokens (not your bank credentials)
- Transaction data for reconciliation
- Account identifiers

### 9.3 Revoking Access

You can disconnect Bank Feed connections at any time through:
- Soledgic dashboard settings
- Your bank's connected apps settings
- Bank Feed Portal (my.bank-feed.com)

### 9.4 Bank Feed's Privacy Policy

Bank Feed has its own privacy policy. Review it at bank-feed.com/legal.
